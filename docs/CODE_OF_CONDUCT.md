# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and respectful community for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive in communication
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith
- Provide actionable technical feedback
- Focus on project outcomes over personal criticism

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to project maintainers through repository issue channels or designated maintainer contact paths.

All reports will be reviewed and handled promptly and fairly.
